### Info générales
Agnès Obel, auteure-compositrice-interprète et musicienne
danoise, née 28 octobre 1980 à Copenhague
Genre musical ? pop alternative
### Jeunesse, débuts
grandi dans un environnement artistique, mère musicienne et père guitariste professionnel jeunesse (+ collectionne des instruments ?) -> influence passion pour musique
apprend à jouer du piano très jeune (4 ans ou 6 ans) 
groupe de rock à 17 ans, joue de la basse

"I truly can't recall a particular memory or moment that made me fall in love with music, but I guess there’s a few. I don't even know what my first memory is. I started playing piano really early, and my piano teacher was a cellist, who also played the instrument. She quickly discovered that I loved a particular repertoire, and the one I was really into was that of Impressionist pianists. I was 10 at the time, and I bought a compact disc with a DVD that played Debussy’s Clair de Lune: I don’t exactly remember the version, but I still have the CD somewhere at home and every time I listen to it, it provides me this out-of-body musical experience. I somewhat realised this piece was the start of a journey."
